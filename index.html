OTTAWA AQUATIC CENTER

About Us

Make a splash, get fit and have fun! The City of Ottawa's Aquatic Centre is the place to be all year round. Whether you choose instructional programs, leisure swim, aquatic leadership or permitting a pool, we have something safe for everyone. Swimming is a great way to maintain lifelong fitness and wellness as it is often ranked as one of the best forms of exercise for its aerobic and resistance training benefits. The Ottawa Aquatic pool operates 6 indoor pools, 4 outdoor pools and plenty of play areas for kids.

Bring your swimsuit, towel and sense of adventure! The Ottawa Aquatic Center offers:

  • · Aquafitness; water sports (basketball, volleyball, underwater hockey, water polo)
  • · Swimming for adults
  • · Early birds
  • · Preschool
  • · Rehab and leisure
  • · Lifesaving and lifeguard courses

We also offer the Red Cross Learn to Swim and First Aid programs. We welcome schools for physical education classes, special occasions and end of the year celebrations. Need a fundraiser? How about a "tread-a-athon" or "slide-a-thon"? And don't forget to bring your friends for a birthday party! Our possibilities are limited only by your imagination!

Swimming is just the beginning... Your new Aquatic Centre is the place to build friendships, confidence, skills and well-being - a healthier community for all.
